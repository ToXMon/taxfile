TASK: NJ Schedule A — Deductions
FILES: taxfile/src/modules/tax-engine/nj/schedule-a.ts
DEPENDS ON: 0033
COMPLEXITY: medium

DESCRIPTION:
NJ Schedule A: uses federal standard or itemized. Medical: 2% floor (NOT 7.5%). Property taxes for deduction/credit election. Miscellaneous: 2% floor.

APPROACH:
Federal base, NJ-specific rules: 2% medical floor, 2% misc floor.

OUTCOME:
NJ Schedule A with NJ-specific rules.

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA:
- Uses federal standard/itemized as base
- Medical 2% floor (not 7.5%)
- Misc 2% floor
- Property taxes separated
- Under 250 lines

DECISIONS LOG:
- NJ medical floor: 2% of NJ gross income (not federal 7.5%) per N.J.S.A. 54A:3-2
- NJ misc floor: 2% of NJ gross income per N.J.S.A. 54A:3-6
- Property taxes separated: deduction (capped at $1,000 from config) vs credit (25% of excess over $100, max $500 from config)
- Flag added when credit > deduction to advise election
- NJ has NO SALT cap on state return (federal $10,000 cap applies only federally)
- NJ charitable contributions: no AGI limits unlike federal IRC §170
- NJ deduction choice: greater of federal standard/itemized OR NJ itemized
- Config-driven: propertyTaxDeductionCap, propertyTaxCreditRate, propertyTaxCreditThreshold, propertyTaxCreditMax from NJTaxConfig
- 161 lines, well under 250 limit

ACCEPTED: all criteria met
